We want to show the world what a real donut tastes like!

Vegans donut futurists believe that in the future, donuts will be totally vegan! Who knows, maybe robots will invent a machine that can turn wheat into dough and chocolate into icing, resulting in the perfect donut for all of us vegans. Or maybe the rise of veganism will result in more options for non-vegans to enjoy donuts. Either way, we're excited to see how this trend plays out. And we're not the only ones excited about the prospect of a vegan donut future. Just take a look at this delicious selection of donut flavors from popular food chains like Dunkin' Donuts and Krispy Kreme: Blueberry Muffin Donut, Cinnamon Sugar Donut, and Funfetti Donut. So if you're ready to make some sweet (and healthy!) culinary waves, get yourself some vegan donuts stat! Read more about our story here.
